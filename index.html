Welcome to Deal Asphalt


At Deal Asphalt Inc. we provide a quality company, our customers can trust. Depending on your asphalt needs, we have crews that are trained and experienced, to do the job right.


Deal Asphalt Inc. is a licensed general paving contractor located in Charlotte North Carolina. We specialize in parking lot maintenance, sealcoating, and patching cracked asphalt. Our main focus is in Charlotte & Spartanburg but are open to travel within S.C. and the southern border of N.C. Just some of the cities we have worked in that involved traveling. N.C. areas - Asheville, Brevard, Charlotte, Denver, Gastonia, Hendersonville, Indian Trail, Monroe, Shelby, Tryon, Union, Waxhaw. S.C. areas - Anderson, Chester, Clemson, Clinton, Columbia, Gaffney, Greenwood, Greenville, Inman, Landrum, Myrtle Beach, Pickens, Spartanburg.


For almost 30 years we have supplied quality asphalt services to the Carolinas. Our staff offers excellent paving and sealcoating services for residents to commercial businesses. Whether its a subdivision, parking lot, driveway, tennis court, golf cart path or walking path our crews are experienced to handle any project no matter what the size.


If you have been looking for one asphalt contractor that is licensed and insured and can handle all of your work out of town, then look no further. Call us today (704) 719-1668 / (864) 473-0194 to schedule an appointment with a free estimate.
